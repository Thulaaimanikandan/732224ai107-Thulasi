# Steps
- Sign in to your automation platform.
- Click Create Workflow.
- Add On form submission as the trigger.
- Add Append row in sheet (Google Sheets).
- Connect the form fields to the sheet columns.
- Execute each step to test.
- Execute the full workflow.
